Spicy Corn Salsa

Description:

This spicy corn salsa is easy to make. It almost tastes like Chipotle's, just a little hotter.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ears sweet Corn
  • 2 poblano peppers
  • red onion, jalapenos, cilantro, lime juice, salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat grill to medium heat and lightly oil.
  2. Grill corn and peppers until soft, 12 to 15 minutes. Let cool.
  3. Cut off corn, dice peppers, mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
